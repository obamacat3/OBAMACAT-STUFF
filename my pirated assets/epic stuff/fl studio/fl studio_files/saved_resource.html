                                                                            Ed Klien Diocares 2 months ago

                                                                            Credits to Helloim Rudz for teaching.

                                                                            1. turn off your wifi and windows defender(click the shield icon at the bottom of your desktop, click virus protection then manage settings. turn off those two buttons that say"real-time protection and cloud covered protection"... now click back arrow twice.. you will see "firewall and network protection" click that and turn off all three firewall protection,domain, private and public..)

                                                                            dont close. leave that window open. you will use it again

                                                                            2. install software, then click the other cube thing that after the install... do not start start fl studio yet..

                                                                            3. go to window with firewall protection settings. here you will advanced settings to make rule to block internet communication between your pc and image-line server.. this communication i why your software goes back to trial mode.

                                                                            4. block internet communications. after you click advanced settings a new window will come up. on the top left you will see "inbound" and "outbound rules". first click inbound rules and look to the top right. click "new rule"

                                                                            a. a new window comes up, click next.

                                                                            b. new window comes up. click browse to find the location of fl studio(c drive/program files/image-line/fl studio 20/)... you will see 4 different exe files for 32/64 bit and scaled versions.. you will make rules for each of them.. start with first one(fl scaled)

                                                                            c. click exe file, then click open at the bottom of window.

                                                                            d. click next..

                                                                            e. new window appears.. click "block connection".. then click next to other wiindow. click next again.

                                                                            f. on this window you will name the "rule" you are creating. i usually name it after the exe file(fl scaled, fl64 etc)

                                                                            g. click "finish" create rule for the exe file.. then do the same for the other three exe files in the inbound section.

                                                                            after all for inbound rules are created you do the same thing in making outbound rules.

                                                                            5. go up to (4) and repeat the steps but do it in the out bound rules section..

                                                                            after all that is done, close the rules window.

                                                                            6. turn on the firewall and virus protection settings you turned off in (1)

                                                                            7. turn on wifi..

                                                                            now all is well, turn on fl studio for the first time..

                                                                            always block internet connection to any cracked software in this same way because the software and the companies that make them can communicate thru your internet connection.. they can put your cracked software back in trial mode, some of them will send virus that will kill your pc.. one company that kills your pc is the on that makes "real guitar"
